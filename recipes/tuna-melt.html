Cheesy Tuna Melt

Description

Put a crunchy, cheesy spin on the classic tuna sandwich with a few simple steps. Serves eight people.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of your preferred bread
  • 1 small sweet onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 (12 oz) can of tuna, drained
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine sweet onion, drained tuna, mozzarella, and mayonnaise. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Spread tuna mixture on slices of bread to form a sandwich. Place sandwiches on a cookie sheet.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven for 10 minutes.

Chorizo Street Tacos

Description

Bring a little bit of deliciousness from the streets of Ciudad de Mexico into your kitchen with these quick and easy-to-make chorizo tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sauasge link, casing removed and meat crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

Bacon Cheeseburger

Description

Delicious, juicy bacon cheeseburgers for everyone at your cookout or game night party. Serves sixteen people.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced bacon, diced
  • 5 pounds ground beef chuck
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup steak sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound American cheese
